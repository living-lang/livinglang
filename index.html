LivingLang is a digital project that brings together a vast collection of historical documents, manuscripts, and codices from around the world. Our mission is to make these resources more easily accessible, and to provide educational tools that are free for all audiences.

Additionally, LivingLang serves as a hub for our ongoing video projects and educational content. Here you can find detailed explanations and discussions behind the scenes, allowing for both accountability and transparency in our efforts.
